Garden Dirt Worms

Description:

Earth Day is coming and instead of the typical green and blue globe shaped treat, I wanted to make something that was a little different and really fun to make with kids! These Jello Worms and dirt are perfect!

Ingredients:

  • worms from the garden
  • raspberry jam
  • 2c organic garden dirt
  • 2T cocoa powder (dirt sweetener)

Steps:

  1. Wait for a rainy weekend
  2. Let the little buddies come out for Sun and snatch them up
  3. Drown dirt worms in raspberry jam for 22 hours
  4. Sift garden dirt with cocoa powder
  5. Take out worms to see if they are still alive, if not place atop garden dirt
  6. Take to your work potluck!
